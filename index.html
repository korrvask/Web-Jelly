Cheyenne, the face behind Web Jelly, is an American-Filipino designer with a heart for contemporary construction. Traveling the world and primarily living in the Philippines has given her valuable experiences that shape her current work. Her designs are driven by a commitment to connection and community, with a strong focus on being intentional and embracing the design process.
“Honestly, I just want to be that breathe of fresh air when it comes to being in the industry. I know I am not the only one who sees the lack of substance within the realm of creators, I care more about the artistic integrity than trying to reach an algorithim.”
- Cheyenne
